The company's original name, <i>American Machine and Metals</i>, was changed to AMETEK in the early 1960s, reflecting AME's evolution from a provider of heavy machinery to a manufacturer of analytical instruments, precision components and specialty materials.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p><p>The firm has two operating groups (the <i>Electronic Instruments Group</i> and the <i>Electromechanical Group</i>).<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> Together, these groups and their divisions comprise over 100 brands, including analytical instruments, monitoring, testing and calibration devices as well as electrical motors, pumps and interconnects.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> The company's headquarters are in <a href="/wiki/Berwyn,_Pennsylvania" title="Berwyn, Pennsylvania">Berwyn, Pennsylvania</a>.

</p>

<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Creaform">Creaform</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ametek&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Creaform" data-section="3" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>Creaform manufactures <a href="/wiki/Metrology" title="Metrology">metrology</a> and <a href="/wiki/3D_modeling" title="3D modeling">3D engineering</a> devices for use in shop-floor environments<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> It is based in <a href="/wiki/L%C3%A9vis,_Quebec" title="Lévis, Quebec">Lévis</a>, <a href="/wiki/Quebec" title="Quebec">Quebec</a>, Canada.  The company was founded in 2002 by Martin Lamontagne and two co-workers at Modelex.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-8">[8]</a></sup> It began with a $200,000 loan from the <a href="/wiki/Business_Development_Bank_of_Canada" title="Business Development Bank of Canada">Business Development Bank of Canada</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-8">[8]</a></sup>  In 2009, the company sold its 1000th laser scanner<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup>, priced from $50 -100,000.<sup id="cite_ref-”Aniwaa”_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-%E2%80%9DAniwaa%E2%80%9D-10">[10]</a></sup> Creaform was acquired by Ametek in 2013 for $120 million in cash.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-11">[11]</a></sup> At the time, Creaform had annual sales of $52 million.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-11">[11]</a></sup> In 2017, Creaform opened a new, $20 million, headquarters in Levis.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-12">[12]</a></sup> The 76,000 square foot  headquarters is double the size of the old one, allowing Creaform to triple its production.
</p>
