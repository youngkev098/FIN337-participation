</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Paycom&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p>Paycom was founded in 1998 by <a href="/wiki/Chad_Richison" title="Chad Richison">Chad Richison</a>, who had formerly worked in the payroll processing industry.<sup id="cite_ref-ExectQA_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ExectQA-10">[10]</a></sup> It began as an online payroll service provider for businesses and expanded to offer additional services including <a href="/wiki/Human_resource_management" title="Human resource management">human resource management</a>, beginning in 2001.<sup id="cite_ref-TheOklaDobles_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TheOklaDobles-2">[2]</a></sup> On April 15, 2014, Paycom opened for trade on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol PAYC.
</p><p>In 2011, the company moved its headquarters to a new 90,000 square foot office building, remaining in Oklahoma City.<sup id="cite_ref-THJrecordpredicts_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-THJrecordpredicts-5">[5]</a></sup> The same year it released additional services including <a href="/wiki/E-Verify" title="E-Verify">E-Verify</a>, <a href="/wiki/Onboarding" title="Onboarding">onboarding</a>, <a href="/wiki/Expense_management" title="Expense management">expense management</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Cloud_storage" title="Cloud storage">document storage</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> In 2014, Paycom built a 90,000-square-foot addition to its headquarters—effectively doubling the size of its offices.<sup id="cite_ref-PaycomBuilt_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PaycomBuilt-12">[12]</a></sup> In 2016, the company completed construction on a third building, bringing the total square-footage of its office space to approximately 250,000.<sup id="cite_ref-PaycomCampus_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PaycomCampus-13">[13]</a></sup> In 2018, Paycom completed construction of its fourth building on its corporate campus totaling an additional 250,000 square-feet and bringing the total size of its headquarters to approximately 500,000 square-feet.<sup id="cite_ref-newsok.com_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-newsok.com-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(2)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="Awards_and_recognition">Awards and recognition</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Paycom&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Awards and recognition" data-section="2" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-2 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-2">
<p>Paycom has been the recipient of numerous awards and recognition including the Service Bureau of the Year Award and being named to the Metro 50 List of Oklahoma companies with the largest growth from the previous two years.<sup id="cite_ref-Esunhonors_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Esunhonors-1">[1]</a></sup> Paycom was named a top workplace in Oklahoma by <a href="/wiki/The_Oklahoman" title="The Oklahoman">The Oklahoman</a> for a fourth consecutive year, topping the list as Oklahoma's No. 1 workplace in 2016.<sup id="cite_ref-PaycomCampus_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PaycomCampus-13">[13]</a></sup> Paycom has also made Deloitte's Technology Fast 500 list for the previous two years.<sup id="cite_ref-PaycomCampus_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PaycomCampus-13">[13]</a></sup></p><p>In 2010, it received a <a href="/wiki/Stevie_Awards" title="Stevie Awards">Stevie Award</a> for Sales Department of the Year in the Financial Services category,<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup> following up with another Stevie Award in 2011 for Best Sales Training Manager of the Year. <i><a href="/wiki/Inc._(magazine)" title="Inc. (magazine)">Inc.</a></i> has recognized Paycom as both a Top 100 Job Creator in 2012 and 2013<sup id="cite_ref-KFORTV4TopJobs_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-KFORTV4TopJobs-15">[15]</a></sup> as well as one of the Fastest-Growing Private Companies for the years 2006 through 2014.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup> In 2014, Paycom was named to the inaugural Build 100, which recognizes less than 1.5 percent of U.S.-based companies that are noted as sustained-growth champions.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup></p><p>Paycom ranked No. 28 among large businesses on <a href="/wiki/Glassdoor" title="Glassdoor">Glassdoor</a>'s 2015 Best Places to Work in the U.S. list.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup> In 2013, Glassdoor named Paycom one of the Top Five Best Places to Work in the U.S.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup> and recognized Paycom again in 2014 as one of the Best Places to Work. Additionally, Paycom ranked in the Top 25 Most Difficult Companies To Interview list compiled by Glassdoor.<sup id="cite_ref-ForbesCompaniesDifficult_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ForbesCompaniesDifficult-20">[20]</a></sup> The same year it received the EPCOR David DeMarea Payments Innovation Award.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">[21]</a></sup></p><p>Paycom won <a href="/wiki/HR.com" title="HR.com">HR.com</a>'s Leadership 500 Excellence Awards in 2015 after earning similar honors in 2014. Paycom earned its place in the midsize company rankings based on its outstanding achievements in leadership development and programs.<sup id="cite_ref-HR.comLeadership_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HR.comLeadership-22">[22]</a></sup></p><p>Paycom was named one of the Wall Street Journal’s Best Managed Companies in 2019.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">[23]</a></sup></p>
