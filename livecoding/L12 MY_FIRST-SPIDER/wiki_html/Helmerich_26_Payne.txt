</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Helmerich_%26_Payne&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p>The company was founded in 1920 as a joint venture between Walt Helmerich II and Bill Payne, who met on an oil rig in <a href="/wiki/South_Bend,_Texas" title="South Bend, Texas">South Bend, Texas</a>. The company moved to Tulsa six years later.<sup id="cite_ref-resistmove_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-resistmove-4">[4]</a></sup></p><p>After benefiting and growing as a result of shale drilling, as of 2015, the company was losing market share and shrinking in size as lower prices in <a href="/wiki/Crude_oil" class="mw-redirect" title="Crude oil">crude oil</a> led to cuts in use of their <a href="/wiki/Drilling_rig" title="Drilling rig">oil rigs</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p><p>In November, 2018, H&amp;P announced that it had formed H&amp;P Technologies, a new business entity that will focus on the development of new technologies, especially for automation of directional drilling applications that can be used on any oil rig, "...regardless of the drilling or service provider.”<sup id="cite_ref-H&amp;P_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-H&amp;P-6">[6]</a></sup> H&amp;P also announced that it had acquired Angus Jamieson Consulting (AJC), of <a href="/wiki/Inverness,_Scotland" class="mw-redirect" title="Inverness, Scotland">Inverness, Scotland</a>, a software-based, training and consultancy company. AJC is widely known as a leader in wellbore positioning, and also provides software and in-depth training for clients.<sup id="cite_ref-H&amp;P_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-H&amp;P-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
