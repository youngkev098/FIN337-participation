</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Globe_Life&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p>The original core of Globe Life, <a href="/wiki/Liberty_National_Life_Insurance_Company" class="mw-redirect" title="Liberty National Life Insurance Company">Liberty National Life Insurance Company</a> was founded in 1900 as the Heralds of Liberty, a fraternal benefit society. The company was headquartered in Birmingham, Alabama until 2006 when it moved to McKinney, Texas.<sup id="cite_ref-marketwatch.com_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-marketwatch.com-5">[5]</a></sup></p><p>In 1980, Liberty National acquired Globe Life And Accident Insurance Company and formed the holding company, Torchmark Corporation.<sup id="cite_ref-ReferenceA_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ReferenceA-6">[6]</a></sup></p><p>In 1981, TMK acquired <a href="/wiki/United_Investors_Life_Insurance_Company" title="United Investors Life Insurance Company">United Investors Life Insurance Company</a> (UIL), United American Insurance Company, and Waddell and Reed Financial.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p><p>In 1994, TMK acquired American Income Life Insurance Company;<sup id="cite_ref-ReferenceA_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ReferenceA-6">[6]</a></sup></p><p>In 1998, TMK spun off <a href="/wiki/Waddell_and_Reed" class="mw-redirect" title="Waddell and Reed">Waddell and Reed Financial</a>, a mutual fund subsidiary.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p><p>In 2006, TMK began moving its headquarters from Birmingham, AL to McKinney, TX.<sup id="cite_ref-marketwatch.com_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-marketwatch.com-5">[5]</a></sup></p><p>In 2010, TMK sold United Investors Life Insurance Company (UIL) to <a href="/wiki/Protective_Life_Corporation" class="mw-redirect" title="Protective Life Corporation">Protective Life Corporation</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p><p>In 2012, TMK acquired Family Heritage Life Insurance Company of America (FHL).<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> It was renamed Globe Life Family Heritage Division in 2019.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-11">[11]</a></sup></p><p>On February 5, 2014, Torchmark's subsidiary Globe Life purchased the naming rights of <a href="/wiki/Globe_Life_Park_in_Arlington" title="Globe Life Park in Arlington">Globe Life Park in Arlington</a>, formerly Rangers Ballpark in Arlington, the home of <a href="/wiki/Major_League_Baseball" title="Major League Baseball">Major League Baseball's</a> <a href="/wiki/Texas_Rangers_Baseball_Club" class="mw-redirect" title="Texas Rangers Baseball Club">Texas Rangers Baseball Club</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Arlington,_TX" class="mw-redirect" title="Arlington, TX">Arlington, TX</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p><p>In August 2001, a mutual fund company filed a civil racketeering suit against Torchmark Corp., which accused Torchmark Corp. and its former chief executive of scheming to continue control over Waddell &amp; Reed Financial Inc. after Torchmark spun it off in 1998.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> However, in a series of orders dated February 20, 2003, August 20, 2004, and September 28, 2004, the United States District Court for the Northern District of Kansas later granted summary judgment against plaintiff Waddell &amp; Reed and in favor of Torchmark and the other defendants named in the action.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup></p><p>On August 8, 2019, Torchmark Corporation was renamed to Globe Life and its ticker symbol changed from "TMK" to "GL".<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup></p>
