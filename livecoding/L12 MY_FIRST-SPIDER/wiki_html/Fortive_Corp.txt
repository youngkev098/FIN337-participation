Fortive was spun off from <a href="/wiki/Danaher_Corporation" title="Danaher Corporation">Danaher</a> in July 2016.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> At the point of its independent incorporation, it immediately became a component of the <a href="/wiki/S%26P_500_Index" title="S&amp;P 500 Index">S&amp;P 500</a>.
