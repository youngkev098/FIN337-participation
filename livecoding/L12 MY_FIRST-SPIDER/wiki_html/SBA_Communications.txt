message</a>)</i></small></div></td></tr></tbody></table><p><b>SBA Communications Corporation</b> owns and operates wireless <a href="/wiki/Infrastructure" title="Infrastructure">infrastructure</a>, including <a href="/wiki/Small_cell" title="Small cell">small cells</a>, indoor/outdoor <a href="/wiki/Distributed_antenna_system" title="Distributed antenna system">distributed antenna systems</a>, and traditional <a href="/wiki/Cell_site" title="Cell site">cell sites</a> that support antennas used for wireless communication by mobile carriers and <a href="/wiki/Wireless_broadband" title="Wireless broadband">wireless broadband</a> providers in the United States and its territories, as well as in <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>, <a href="/wiki/Central_America" title="Central America">Central America</a>, and <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">South America</a>. The company operates in two segments, Site Leasing and Site Development. SBA Communications Corporation leases antenna space to wireless service providers on towers that it owns or operates; and manages rooftop and tower sites for property owners under various contractual arrangements. As of December 31, 2013, it owned 20,079 towers.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2019)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> The company also managed or leased approximately 4,800 actual or potential towers. In addition, it provides various site development services comprising network pre-design; site audits; identification of potential locations for towers and antennas; support in buying or leasing of the location; assistance in obtaining zoning approvals and permits; tower structure construction; antenna installation; and radio equipment installation, commissioning, and maintenance. SBA Communications Corporation was founded in 1989 and is headquartered in Boca Raton, Florida.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The current CEO is Jeffrey A.
