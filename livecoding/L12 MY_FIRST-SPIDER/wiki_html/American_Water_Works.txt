<p>Most of American Water's <a href="/wiki/Public_services" class="mw-redirect" title="Public services">services</a> are locally managed utility subsidiaries that are regulated by the <a href="/wiki/U.S._state" title="U.S. state">U.S. state</a> in which each operates. American Water also owns subsidiaries that manage municipal <a href="/wiki/Drinking_water" title="Drinking water">drinking water</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wastewater" title="Wastewater">wastewater</a> systems under contract and others that supply businesses and residential communities with water management products and services. In 2003, American Water established the American Water Military Services Group,  which serves as a contracted <a href="/wiki/Water_utility" class="mw-redirect" title="Water utility">water utility</a> for several military bases in the United States. Its Military Services Group works with the United States Military to treat and supply water and to collect and treat wastewater for military installations all over the country. To date, American Water Military Services Group provides services to 11 military bases.
</p><p>American Water Works Company provides services such as water line protection, sewer line protection, an in-home plumbing emergency program, electric line protection, heating system protection, cooling system protection, power surge protection, and water heater protection.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p><p>With headquarters in <a href="/wiki/Camden,_New_Jersey" title="Camden, New Jersey">Camden, New Jersey</a>, American Water has about 7,100 employees and provides services to approximately 14 million people in 46 U.S. states. <sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p><p>As of 2017 American Water shares are mainly held by institutional investors (<a href="/wiki/Vanguard_Group" class="mw-redirect" title="Vanguard Group">Vanguard Group</a>, <a href="/wiki/BlackRock" title="BlackRock">BlackRock</a>, <a href="/wiki/State_Street_Corporation" title="State Street Corporation">State Street Corporation</a>, and others.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup>)
</p>
