</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History_and_operations">History and operations</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Packaging_Corporation_of_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History and operations" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p>The company was founded in 1959 by the consolidation of three companies and was eventually acquired by <a href="/wiki/Tenneco" title="Tenneco">Tenneco</a> in 1965.
</p><p>In November 1995, the company's name was changed to <b>Tenneco Packaging Inc.</b>; in 1999, a new entity re-established the name <b>Packaging Corporation of America</b>.
</p><p>In 2009, the company opened a new design center and project-management facility in Hong Kong, operating under the name Packaging Corporation of Asia Limited.
</p><p>In October 2013, the company acquired Boise Inc (this is different from Boise Cascade).
</p><p>In 2016, the company acquired Timbar Packaging and Display, an independent corrugated manufacturer with multiple locations in the eastern U.S. and Columbus Container, an independent corrugated manufacturer with multiple locations in Indiana. 
</p><p>In 2017, the company acquired Sacramento Container Corporation, an independent corrugated manufacturer with multiple locations in Northern California.
</p><p>In 2018, the company acquired Englander Container and Display, with sheet plants in Waco and Carrolton, Texas, and a design center in Bentonville, Arkansas
</p><p>The company's network consists of 6 containerboard mills, 2 white paper mills, 95 converting facilities, 10 creative design centers, 3 "fulfillment centers"<sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="margin-left:0.1em; white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Please_clarify" title="Wikipedia:Please clarify"><span title="The text near this tag may need clarification or removal of jargon. (July 2018)">clarification needed</span></a></i>]</sup>, 8 packaging and supply centers, 2 training and resource centers and 1 technical center.
</p>
