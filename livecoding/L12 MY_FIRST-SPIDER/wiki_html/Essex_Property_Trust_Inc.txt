<p>The company was founded in 1971 by <a href="/wiki/Billionaire" title="Billionaire">billionaire</a> <a href="/wiki/George_M._Marcus" title="George M. Marcus">George M. Marcus</a>, who also founded <a href="/wiki/Marcus_%26_Millichap" title="Marcus &amp; Millichap">Marcus &amp; Millichap</a> that same year.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p><p>On June 13, 1994, the company became a <a href="/wiki/Public_company" title="Public company">public company</a> via an <a href="/wiki/Initial_public_offering" title="Initial public offering">initial public offering</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-10K_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10K-1">[1]</a></sup></p><p>In 2014, the company acquired BRE Properties for $4.3 billion.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> The same year it was added to the <a href="/wiki/S%26P_500_Index" title="S&amp;P 500 Index">S&amp;P 500 Index</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
