			<div id="bodyContent" class="content"><div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><div class="mw-parser-output"><section class="mf-section-0" id="mf-section-0"><p><b>IDEXX Laboratories, Inc.</b> is an American  <a href="/wiki/Multinational_corporation" title="Multinational corporation">multinational corporation</a>  <sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> engaged in the development, manufacture, and distribution of products and services for the companion animal veterinary, livestock and poultry, water testing, and dairy markets. Incorporated in 1983 and headquartered in <a href="/wiki/Westbrook,_Maine" title="Westbrook, Maine">Westbrook, Maine</a>, and EMEA in <a href="/wiki/Hoofddorp,_Netherlands" class="mw-redirect" title="Hoofddorp, Netherlands">Hoofddorp, Netherlands</a>,
IDEXX offers products to customers in over 175 countries around the world and employs over 7,000 people in full-and part-time positions (as of 31 December 2017<sup class="plainlinks noexcerpt noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Idexx_Laboratories&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup>).<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> There are three main segments of the company: Companion Animal Group, Water, and Livestock, Poultry and Dairy (LPD). In addition, the company also manufactures and sells pet-side SNAP tests for a variety of animal health diagnostic uses.

</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span id="Products_.26_Services"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Products_&amp;_Services">Products &amp; Services</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Idexx_Laboratories&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Products &amp; Services" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Companion_Animal_Group">Companion Animal Group</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Idexx_Laboratories&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Companion Animal Group" data-section="2" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>The Companion Animal Group ("CAG") provides in-clinic laboratory <a href="/wiki/Analyzer" class="mw-redirect" title="Analyzer">analyzers</a> for companion animals. Analyzers measure blood cell counts as well as levels of certain <a href="/wiki/Enzyme" title="Enzyme">enzymes</a> in blood or urine for the purpose of monitoring health conditions. Major products of this segment are the Catalyst Dx Chemistry Analyzer and the VetTest Chemistry Analyzer, Symmetric dimethylarginine (SDMA),<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> ProCyte Dx Hematology Analyzer, the LaserCyte Hematology Analyzer, the IDEXX VetAutoread Hematology Analyzer, the SNAP Pro analyzer, and so on, among other laboratory equipment and analytical services.  Other areas of the CAG division are centered on <a href="/wiki/Diagnostic_imaging" class="mw-redirect" title="Diagnostic imaging">diagnostic imaging</a> and veterinary practice management software systems, including the flagship program Cornerstone, as well as DVMax (acquired from Sneakers Software, Inc., a New York City-based company, under terms of <a href="/wiki/Chapter_11,_Title_11,_United_States_Code" title="Chapter 11, Title 11, United States Code">Ch. 11</a> reorganization <a rel="nofollow" class="external autonumber" href="http://dvmax.com/P/Reorganization02.html">[2]</a>) in late 2012 <a rel="nofollow" class="external autonumber" href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140512214109/http://www.idexx.com/view/xhtml/en_us/corporate/news/press-releases/20121220pr.jsf">[3]</a>), Animana and several legacy practice management software systems.
</p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Water_testing">Water testing</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Idexx_Laboratories&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Water testing" data-section="3" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>The Water segment provides products to measure microbiological parameters in water. Products of this segment include the Colilert, Colilert-18, and Colisure tests. Water products are sold in over 120 countries around the globe.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Livestock_and_Poultry_Diagnostics">Livestock and Poultry Diagnostics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Idexx_Laboratories&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Livestock and Poultry Diagnostics" data-section="4" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>The Livestock and Poultry Diagnostics division provides products to detect diseases in livestock and poultry. Products of this segment include tests for Avian Influenza (AI), Bovine Viral Diarrhoea Virus (BVDV), Porcine Reproductive and Respiratory Syndrome (PRRS), and Bovine Spongiform Encephalopathy (BSE or mad cow disease).<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
