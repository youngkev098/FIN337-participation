<p>In 1962, the company was founded by Samuel J. Gorlitz in <a href="/wiki/Washington,_D.C." title="Washington, D.C.">Washington, D.C.</a><sup id="cite_ref-Gorlitz_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Gorlitz-2">[2]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-10K_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10K-1">[1]</a></sup></p><p>In 1999, the company was reorganized as a real estate investment trust in <a href="/wiki/Maryland" title="Maryland">Maryland</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-10K_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10K-1">[1]</a></sup></p><p>In 2007, founder Samuel J. Gorlitz died.<sup id="cite_ref-Gorlitz_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Gorlitz-2">[2]</a></sup></p><p>In 2008, the company acquired Del Mar Village in <a href="/wiki/Boca_Raton,_Florida" title="Boca Raton, Florida">Boca Raton, Florida</a> for $41.7 million.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p><p>In 2012, the company acquired a controlling interest in Montrose Crossing, a property in Rockville, Maryland for $127 million<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> and a controlling interest in a property in <a href="/wiki/El_Segundo,_California" title="El Segundo, California">El Segundo, California</a> for $183.5 million.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p><p>In 2013, the company sold a <a href="/wiki/Movie_theater" title="Movie theater">movie theater</a> in <a href="/wiki/Forest_Hills,_New_York" class="mw-redirect" title="Forest Hills, New York">Forest Hills, New York</a> for $20.5 million.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p><p>In 2014, the company acquired 2 shopping centers in <a href="/wiki/Monmouth_County,_New_Jersey" title="Monmouth County, New Jersey">Monmouth County, New Jersey</a> for $161 million.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p><p>In 2015, the company acquired an 85% interest in <a href="/wiki/The_Shops_at_Sunset_Place" title="The Shops at Sunset Place">The Shops at Sunset Place</a> based on a property valuation of $110.2 million.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p><p>In 2016, the company was added to the <a href="/wiki/S%26P_500_Index"
