<p><b>Discover Financial Services, Inc.</b> is an American <a href="/wiki/Financial_services" title="Financial services">financial services</a> company that owns and operates Discover Bank, which offers <a href="/wiki/Checking_account" class="mw-redirect" title="Checking account">checking</a> and <a href="/wiki/Savings_account" title="Savings account">savings accounts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Personal_loans" class="mw-redirect" title="Personal loans">personal loans</a>, <a href="/wiki/Home_equity_loan" title="Home equity loan">home equity loans</a>, <a href="/wiki/Student_loan" title="Student loan">student loans</a> and <a href="/wiki/Credit_card" title="Credit card">credit cards</a>. It also owns and operates the Discover and <a href="/wiki/Pulse_(interbank_network)" title="Pulse (interbank network)">Pulse</a> <a href="/wiki/Interbank_network" title="Interbank network">networks</a>, and owns <a href="/wiki/Diners_Club_International" title="Diners Club International">Diners Club International</a>. <a href="/wiki/Discover_Card" title="Discover Card">Discover Card</a> is the third largest <a href="/wiki/Credit_card" title="Credit card">credit card</a> brand in the United States, when measured by cards in force, with nearly 50 million cardholders. </section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Discover_Financial&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p><a href="/wiki/Sears,_Roebuck_and_Company" class="mw-redirect" title="Sears, Roebuck and Company">Sears</a> introduced the Discover credit card in 1985 in conjunction with real estate franchise <a href="/wiki/Coldwell_Banker" title="Coldwell Banker">Coldwell, Banker &amp; Company</a> and brokerage firm <a href="/wiki/Dean_Witter_Reynolds" title="Dean Witter Reynolds">Dean Witter Reynolds</a>. Altogether, these companies operated under the banner Sears Financial Network. In 1993, Sears sold off its financial services branch with Dean Witter Reynolds in charge of Discover. This independent company was called Dean Witter, Discover &amp; Co. In 1997, Dean Witter, Discover &amp; Co. merged with investment banking house <a href="/wiki/Morgan_Stanley" title="Morgan Stanley">Morgan Stanley</a>. In December 2006, Morgan Stanley announced it would spin off Discover by the end of August 2007. On June 30, 2007, Discover was <a href="/wiki/Corporate_spin-off" title="Corporate spin-off">spun off</a> as an independent, publicly traded company called Discover Financial Services, Inc.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (February 2020)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p><p>On December 21, 2001, fourth-quarter income at Discover grew 31% to $193 million, compared with $147 million the year before.<sup id="cite_ref-lexis_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lexis-4">[4]</a></sup></p><p>On March 5, 2002, the company announced that the Account Center at Discovercard.com had registered over eight million card members, an increase of 61% since January 2001.<sup id="cite_ref-buswire_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-buswire-5">[5]</a></sup></p><p>On September 1, 2002, Discover announced that Citibank would accept the Discover® Card for cash advances at its approximately 450 Financial Centers and more than 2,000 ATM locations nationwide.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p><p>On March 19, 2020, amidst the Corona virus pandemic, Discover announced that they would be raising their late payment and bounced check fees beginning on June 1, 2020<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (April 2020)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Discover_acquires_The_Greenwood_Trust_Company">Discover acquires The Greenwood Trust Company</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Discover_Financial&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Discover acquires The Greenwood Trust Company" data-section="2" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>The Greenwood Trust Company was founded in 1911 and was based in <a href="/wiki/Greenwood,_Delaware" title="Greenwood, Delaware">Greenwood, Delaware</a>. It was acquired by Discover in 1985 and renamed Discover Bank in 2000.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> Discover Bank is mainly an online financial institution that offers many financial services such as checking accounts, savings accounts, and money market accounts. However the first and original location of The Greenwood Trust Company on East Market Street is still operating and is the only physical banking location of Discover Bank.
</p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Discover_acquires_Pulse">Discover acquires Pulse</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Discover_Financial&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Discover acquires Pulse" data-section="3" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>In 2005, Discover acquired Pulse, an interbank electronic funds transfer network. With this acquisition, Discover obtained the ability to market and issue debit and ATM cards. Starting in February 2006, Discover announced that it would begin offering Discover Debit, a <a href="/wiki/Debit_card" title="Debit card">debit</a> product, to other financial institutions.
</p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Discover_acquires_Diners_Club_International">Discover acquires Diners Club International</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Discover_Financial&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Discover acquires Diners Club International" data-section="4" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>In April 2008, Discover Financial and <a href="/wiki/Citigroup" title="Citigroup">Citigroup</a> announced that Discover was purchasing the <a href="/wiki/Diners_Club_International" title="Diners Club International">Diners Club International</a> network from Citigroup for $165 million. In May 2008, the <a href="/wiki/Federal_Trade_Commission" title="Federal Trade Commission">Federal Trade Commission</a> approved the transaction and it finalized on July 1, 2008.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> Discover stated that the Diners Club network, which is a major network outside the United States, will be merged with the Discover Network, a major network in the United States, creating an international network for Discover and Diners Club cardholders. Diners Club cards will continue to be issued by Diners Club International licensees. At this time, Discover Bank has no plans on issuing Diners Club branded cards itself.
</p><p>On September 17, 2010, Discover Financial announced it would acquire Citigroup-owned Student Loan Corporation for $30/share.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> The deal finalized on December 31.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Discover_acquires_Home_Loan_Center">Discover acquires Home Loan Center</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Discover_Financial&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Discover acquires Home Loan Center" data-section="5" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>On June 6, 2012, Tree.com, Inc. completed the sale of substantially all of the operating assets of its Home Loan Center, Inc. business to a wholly owned subsidiary of Discover Financial. In exchange for the assets sold, Discover paid an aggregate of $45.9 million including payments made prior to the closing which were applied to the closing price.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup>  On June 12, 2012, Discover began originating mortgages with its launch of Discover Home Loans, offering prime variable- and fixed-rate conventional and FHA home mortgage loans.
</p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Discover_and_TARP">Discover and TARP</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Discover_Financial&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Discover and TARP" data-section="6" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>Following the <a href="/wiki/Financial_crisis_of_2007-08" class="mw-redirect" title="Financial crisis of 2007-08">2007-08 financial crisis</a>, Discover received about $1.2 billion in bailout funds under the <a href="/wiki/Troubled_Asset_Relief_Program" title="Troubled Asset Relief Program">Troubled Asset Relief Program</a> (TARP). The company announced in 2010 that it had received approval to pay back the funds.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(2)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="Competitors">Competitors</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Discover_Financial&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Competitors" data-section="7" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-2 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-2">
<p>In the United States, Discover, as a card brand, competes primarily with <a href="/wiki/Visa_(company)" class="mw-redirect" title="Visa (company)">Visa</a>, <a href="/wiki/MasterCard" class="mw-redirect" title="MasterCard">MasterCard</a>, and <a href="/wiki/American_Express" title="American Express">American Express</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> Unlike <a href="/wiki/Visa_Inc." title="Visa Inc.">Visa</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mastercard" title="Mastercard">MasterCard</a>, Discover directly issues its cards, through its Discover Bank unit.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup> When measured by card balances, Discover is the sixth largest credit card issuer in the U.S. behind <a href="/wiki/JPMorgan_Chase" title="JPMorgan Chase">JPMorgan Chase</a>, <a href="/wiki/Citigroup" title="Citigroup">Citigroup</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bank_of_America" title="Bank of America">Bank of America</a>, <a href="/wiki/Capital_One" title="Capital One">Capital One</a> and <a href="/wiki/American_Express" title="American Express">American Express</a>, and ahead of <a href="/wiki/Wells_Fargo" title="Wells Fargo">Wells Fargo</a> and <a href="/wiki/U.S._Bancorp" title="U.S. Bancorp">U.S. Bank</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup></p>
</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(3)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="Business">Business</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Discover_Financial&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Business" data-section="8" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-3 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-3">
<p>In addition to its card products, Discover also markets <a href="/wiki/Saving_(money)" class="mw-redirect" title="Saving (money)">savings products</a>, <a href="/wiki/Transaction_account" title="Transaction account">checking accounts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Personal_loan" class="mw-redirect" title="Personal loan">personal loans</a>, <a href="/wiki/Student_loans_in_the_United_States" title="Student loans in the United States">student loans</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Home_equity_loan" title="Home equity loan">home equity loans</a>.
</p>
