</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Skyworks_Solutions&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p>The company formed as a result of a merger of Alpha Industries and the wireless communications division of <a href="/wiki/Conexant" title="Conexant">Conexant</a>, which took effect on June 26, 2002.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Headquartered in <a href="/wiki/Irvine,_California" title="Irvine, California">Irvine, California</a>, Skyworks has manufacturing facilities in <a href="/wiki/Woburn,_Massachusetts" title="Woburn, Massachusetts">Woburn, Massachusetts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Newbury_Park,_California" title="Newbury Park, California">Newbury Park, California</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mexicali,_Mexico" class="mw-redirect" title="Mexicali, Mexico">Mexicali, Mexico</a>. It has design centers in Irvine; <a href="/wiki/Santa_Rosa,_California" title="Santa Rosa, California">Santa Rosa, California</a>; Newbury Park; Woburn; <a href="/wiki/Greensboro,_North_Carolina" title="Greensboro, North Carolina">Greensboro, North Carolina</a>; <a href="/wiki/Cedar_Rapids,_Iowa" title="Cedar Rapids, Iowa">Cedar Rapids, Iowa</a>; and <a href="/wiki/Ottawa,_Ontario" class="mw-redirect" title="Ottawa, Ontario">Ottawa, Ontario</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-locations_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-locations-4">[4]</a></sup> According to its website, the company has design, engineering, manufacturing, marketing, sales and service facilities throughout <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">North America</a>, <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>, Japan and <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">Asia</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-locations_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-locations-4">[4]</a></sup></p><p>The music video for <a href="/wiki/The_Postal_Service" title="The Postal Service">The Postal Service</a>'s song "<a href="/wiki/Such_Great_Heights" title="Such Great Heights">Such Great Heights</a>" is set in Skyworks' Newbury Park chip fabrication plant.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p><p>On October 5, 2015, Skyworks Solutions entered a definitive agreement to acquire <a href="/wiki/PMC-Sierra" title="PMC-Sierra">PMC-Sierra</a> for $2 billion in cash.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> However, Skyworks walked away from the deal, having been outbid by <a href="/wiki/Microsemi" title="Microsemi">Microsemi</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2018)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
