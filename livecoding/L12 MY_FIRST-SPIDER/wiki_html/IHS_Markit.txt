</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=IHS_Markit&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p>One part of this <a href="/wiki/Conglomerate_(company)" title="Conglomerate (company)">conglomerate</a> originally was a firm that assigns <a href="/wiki/IMO_number" title="IMO number">IMO identification numbers</a> for ships, companies and registered owners.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> It has since grown to incorporate other companies in the information services sector, many dating back to the late 1700s and 1800s. These include <a href="/wiki/Cambridge_Energy_Research_Associates" title="Cambridge Energy Research Associates">Cambridge Energy Research Associates</a>, <a href="/wiki/Global_Insight" title="Global Insight">Global Insight</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jane%27s_Information_Group" title="Jane's Information Group">Jane's Information Group</a>, <a href="/wiki/Carfax_(company)" title="Carfax (company)">Carfax, Inc.</a>, Prime Publications Limited, and John S. Herold, Inc. 
</p><p>In 2016 <a href="/wiki/Englewood,_Colorado" title="Englewood, Colorado">Englewood, Colorado</a>–based IHS and London-based Markit merged.  Information Handling Services (IHS)<sup id="cite_ref-IHS1959B_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IHS1959B-9">[9]</a></sup> "was founded in 1959 as Information Handling Services to provide information for aerospace engineers through microfilm databases."<sup id="cite_ref-Picker_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Picker-10">[10]</a></sup> Jerre Stead was the chief executive from 2006 to 2013 and from 2015 until the merger with Markit.<sup id="cite_ref-Picker_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Picker-10">[10]</a></sup></p><p><a href="/wiki/Markit" title="Markit">Markit</a> was founded in 2003 as Mark-it Partners, a financial data provider for daily <a href="/wiki/Credit_default_swap" title="Credit default swap">credit default swap</a> pricing.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> The company grew via <a href="/wiki/Joint_venture" title="Joint venture">joint ventures</a> and by acquiring other companies,<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup> merging with IHS in 2016.
</p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="1959">1959</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=IHS_Markit&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1959" data-section="2" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>IHS's founding date refers to a 1959-initiated publication about <a href="/wiki/Microfilm" class="mw-redirect" title="Microfilm">microfilm</a><sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup> begun by an <a href="/wiki/Englewood,_Colorado" title="Englewood, Colorado">Englewood, Colorado</a>-based former <a href="/wiki/RELX" title="RELX">RELX</a>-
precursor which <a href="/wiki/Norman_Cahners" title="Norman Cahners">Norman L. Cahners</a>'<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup> 1945-initiated publishing empire, led by <a href="/wiki/Modern_Materials_Handling" title="Modern Materials Handling">Modern Materials Handling</a><sup id="cite_ref-Brewer2015_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Brewer2015-20">[20]</a></sup> acquired and expanded.
</p>
