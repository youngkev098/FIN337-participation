<p><b>Dow Inc.</b> is an American <a href="/wiki/Commodity_chemical" class="mw-redirect" title="Commodity chemical">commodity chemical</a> company. It was spun off of <a href="/wiki/DowDuPont" class="mw-redirect" title="DowDuPont">DowDuPont</a> on April 1, 2019, at which time it became a <a href="/wiki/Public_company" title="Public company">public company</a> and was added to the <a href="/wiki/Dow_Jones_Industrial_Average" title="Dow Jones Industrial Average">Dow Jones Industrial Average</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The company is headquartered in <a href="/wiki/Midland,_Michigan" title="Midland, Michigan">Midland, Michigan</a>.
