Please discuss further on the <a href="/wiki/Talk:Synopsys" title="Talk:Synopsys">talk page</a>.</span>  <small class="date-container"><i>(<span class="date">February 2020</span>)</i></small><small class="hide-when-compact"><i> (<a href="/wiki/Help:Maintenance_template_removal" title="Help:Maintenance template removal">Learn how and when to remove this template message</a>)</i></small></div></td></tr></tbody></table><p><b>Synopsys</b> (<span class="rt-commentedText nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups noexcerpt"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/English" title="Help:IPA/English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="'s' in 'sigh'">s</span><span title="/ɪ/: 'i' in 'kit'">ɪ</span><span title="/ˈ/: primary stress follows">ˈ</span><span title="'n' in 'nigh'">n</span><span title="/ɒ/: 'o' in 'body'">ɒ</span><span title="'p' in 'pie'">p</span><span title="'s' in 'sigh'">s</span><span title="/ɪ/: 'i' in 'kit'">ɪ</span><span title="'s' in 'sigh'">s</span></span>/</a></span></span>) is an American <a href="/wiki/Electronic_design_automation" title="Electronic design automation">electronic design automation</a> company that focuses on silicon design and verification, silicon <a href="/wiki/Intellectual_property" title="Intellectual property">intellectual property</a> and <a href="/wiki/Computer_security" title="Computer security">software security</a> and quality.  Products include <a href="/wiki/Logic_synthesis" title="Logic synthesis">logic synthesis</a>, behavioral synthesis, <a href="/wiki/Place_and_route" title="Place and route">place and route</a>, <a href="/wiki/Static_timing_analysis" title="Static timing analysis">static timing analysis</a>, <a href="/wiki/Formal_verification" title="Formal verification">formal verification</a>, hardware description language (<a href="/wiki/SystemC" title="SystemC">SystemC</a>, <a href="/wiki/SystemVerilog" title="SystemVerilog">SystemVerilog</a>/<a href="/wiki/Verilog" title="Verilog">Verilog</a>, <a href="/wiki/VHDL" title="VHDL">VHDL</a>) simulators, as well as transistor-level <a href="/wiki/Circuit_simulation" class="mw-redirect" title="Circuit simulation">circuit simulation</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Simulation" title="Simulation">simulators</a> include development and <a href="/wiki/Debugging" title="Debugging">debugging</a> environments which assist in the design of the logic for <a href="/wiki/Integrated_circuit" title="Integrated circuit">chips</a> and computer systems. In recent years, Synopsys has expanded its products and services to include <a href="/wiki/Application_security" title="Application security">application security</a> testing. Their technology is present in <a href="/wiki/Self-driving_car" title="Self-driving car">self-driving cars</a>, <a href="/wiki/Artificial_intelligence" title="Artificial intelligence">artificial intelligence</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Internet_of_things" title="Internet of things">internet of things</a> consumer products.

</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Synopsys&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p>Synopsys was founded by <a href="/wiki/Aart_de_Geus" title="Aart de Geus">Aart J de Geus</a> and David Gregory in 1986 in Research Triangle Park, North Carolina. The outfit was initially established as Optimal Solutions with a charter to develop and market synthesis technology developed by the team at <a href="/wiki/General_Electric" title="General Electric">General Electric</a>. They have evolved to become a leader in electronic design automation, semiconductor intellectual property, and software security solutions.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (February 2020)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(2)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="Divisions">Divisions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Synopsys&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Divisions" data-section="2" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-2 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-2">
<p>Synopsys has three divisions including silicon design and verification, silicon intellectual property, and software integrity.
</p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span id="Silicon_Design_.26_Verification"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Silicon_Design_&amp;_Verification">Silicon Design &amp; Verification</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Synopsys&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Silicon Design &amp; Verification" data-section="3" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>This Synopsys division focuses the design and verification of integrated circuits and designing more advanced processes and models for the manufacturing of those chips.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Silicon_Intellectual_Property">Silicon Intellectual Property</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Synopsys&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Silicon Intellectual Property" data-section="4" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>This division of Synopsys focuses on the enabling organizations to create high-quality silicon proven intellectual property solutions for System on a chip (SoC) designs.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Software_Integrity">Software Integrity</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Synopsys&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Software Integrity" data-section="5" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>In 2014, Synopsys began to expand their products and services to include software security and quality. This division helps organizations integrate security into <a href="/wiki/DevOps" title="DevOps">DevOps</a> environments, build holistic <a href="/wiki/Application_security" title="Application security">application security</a> programs, <a href="/wiki/Software_testing" title="Software testing">test any software</a> on-demand, find and fix <a href="/wiki/Software_licensing_audit" title="Software licensing audit">software quality and compliance</a> issues earlier, identify and manage <a href="/wiki/Open-source_software" title="Open-source software">open source</a> components, and assess application security threats, risks and dependancies.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(3)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="Products">Products</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Synopsys&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Products" data-section="6" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-3 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-3">
