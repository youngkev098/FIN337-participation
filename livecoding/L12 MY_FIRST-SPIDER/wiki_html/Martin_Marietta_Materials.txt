The company is a supplier of <a href="/wiki/Aggregate_(composite)" title="Aggregate (composite)">aggregates</a> and heavy <a href="/wiki/Building_materials" class="mw-redirect" title="Building materials">building materials</a>, with operations spanning 26 states, Canada and the <a href="/wiki/Caribbean" title="Caribbean">Caribbean</a>. In particular, Martin Marietta supplies the resources for roads, sidewalks and foundations.

<p>Martin Marietta's Magnesia Specialties business provides a full range of <a href="/wiki/Magnesium_oxide" title="Magnesium oxide">magnesium oxide</a>, <a href="/wiki/Magnesium_hydroxide" title="Magnesium hydroxide">magnesium hydroxide</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lime_(material)" title="Lime (material)">dolomitic lime</a> products.
</p><p>It was established as an independent company in 1996, spun off from the newly created <a href="/wiki/Lockheed_Martin" title="Lockheed Martin">Lockheed Martin</a> after having been part of <a href="/wiki/Martin_Marietta" title="Martin Marietta">Martin Marietta</a> since 1961. It dates its origins back to 1939, when <a href="/w/index.php?title=Superior_Stone&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Superior Stone (page does not exist)">Superior Stone</a>, an aggregates company in <a href="/wiki/Raleigh,_North_Carolina" title="Raleigh, North Carolina">Raleigh, North Carolina</a>, was founded.
</p>
