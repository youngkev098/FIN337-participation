</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="Mergers_and_subsidiaries">Mergers and subsidiaries</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allegion&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Mergers and subsidiaries" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p>On 8 January 2014, Allegion acquired certain assets of Schlage Lock de Colombia S.A., the second largest mechanical lock manufacturer in <a href="/wiki/Colombia" title="Colombia">Colombia</a>, with revenues of US$12 million. The acquisition gave Allegion a 45,000 square foot integrated plant in <a href="/wiki/Bogot%C3%A1" title="Bogotá">Bogota, Colombia</a>, and approximately 350 new employees.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(2)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="Ownership">Ownership</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allegion&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Ownership" data-section="2" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-2 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-2">
<p>As of 2017, Allegion shares are mainly held by institutional investors (<a href="/wiki/T._Rowe_Price" title="T. Rowe Price">T. Rowe Price</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vanguard_group" class="mw-redirect" title="Vanguard group">Vanguard Group</a>, <a href="/wiki/JPMorgan_Chase" title="JPMorgan Chase">JPMorgan Chase</a> and others)<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(3)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="Locations">Locations</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allegion&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Locations" data-section="3" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-3 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-3">
<p>Allegion plc is headquartered in <a href="/wiki/Dublin" title="Dublin">Dublin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>. Its regional offices for the Americas is in <a href="/wiki/Carmel,_Indiana" title="Carmel, Indiana">Carmel, Indiana</a>, United States. Its European regional offices are in <a href="/wiki/Faenza" title="Faenza">Faenza</a>, <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>, and the company's Asia headquarters are in Tower B of City Center of Shanghai in <a href="/wiki/Shanghai" title="Shanghai">Shanghai</a>, <a href="/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China" class="mw-redirect" title="People's Republic of China">China</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>

</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(5)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="Ventures">Ventures</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allegion&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Ventures" data-section="5" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-5 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-5">
<p>In March 2018, the company launched the venture capital fund, <i>Allegion Ventures</i>. The aim is to invest in early stage companies in the sectors security and smart access. The initial funding was $50 million.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> The following companies are part of the Allegion Ventures Programme:<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
