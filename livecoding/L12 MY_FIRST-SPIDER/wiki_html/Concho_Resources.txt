</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Concho_Resources&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p>In 2004, the company was founded as Concho Equity Holdings Corporation by a group of businessmen headed Timothy A. Leach. 
</p><p>Also in 2004, the company acquired properties from Lowe Management for $117 million.
</p><p>In 2006, the company acquired assets from Chase Oil and formed Concho Resources Inc.
</p><p>In 2007, the company became a <a href="/wiki/Public_company" title="Public company">public company</a> via an <a href="/wiki/Initial_public_offering" title="Initial public offering">initial public offering</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p><p>In 2008, the company acquired Henry Petroleum for $584 million.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p><p>In 2010, the company acquired assets in the Permian Basin from Marbob Energy Corporation for $1.65 billion.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p><p>In 2012, the company acquired Three Rivers Operating Company for $1 billion.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p><p>In October 2016, the company acquired assets in the Midland Basin from Reliance Energy for $1.625 billion.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p><p>In November 2016, the company acquired assets in the Delaware Basin for $430 million.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p><p>In July 2018, the company acquired RSP Permian.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
