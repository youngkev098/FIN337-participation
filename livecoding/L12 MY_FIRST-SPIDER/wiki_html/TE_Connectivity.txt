<p>In 1941, Aircraft and Marine Products (AMP) was founded with solderless electrical connections for quick and removable wire connection used for aircraft and ships.  After the war time boom the company had to adapt to post war economy and in 1956 the name was changed to AMP Incorporated when it incorporated.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> In 1999, Tyco International acquired American electronics connector manufacturer AMP Incorporated.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p><p>In September 2002 the CEO (<a href="/wiki/Dennis_Kozlowski" title="Dennis Kozlowski">L. Dennis Kozlowski</a>) and CFO (Mark H. Swartz) of Tyco International Ltd. were indicted on charges including fraud, racketeering, stock manipulation, and more, amounting to more than $600 million dollars of theft <sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p><p>In July 2007, Tyco separated into three publicly independent companies, <a href="/wiki/Covidien" title="Covidien">Covidien</a> Ltd (formerly Tyco Healthcare), Tyco Electronics Ltd, and <a href="/wiki/Tyco_International" title="Tyco International">Tyco International</a> Ltd (formerly Tyco Fire &amp; Security and Tyco Engineered Products &amp; Services (TFS/TEPS)).<sup id="cite_ref-History_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-History-7">[7]</a></sup></p><p>On March 10, 2011, Tyco Electronics Ltd changed its name to TE Connectivity Ltd, which the company said felt more relevant to its position as a connectivity and sensor component manufacturer.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p><p>On August 28, 2015, TE Connectivity announced that it has completed the sale of its broadband-networks business to <a href="/wiki/CommScope" title="CommScope">CommScope</a> Holding Co. for about US$3 billion.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(2)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="Products_and_Services">Products and Services</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=TE_Connectivity&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Products and Services" data-section="2" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-2 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-2">
<p>TE Connectivity's product portfolio is focused on connectors and sensors<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> that are made to withstand harsh environments.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> The company operates three primary segments:<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Communications_Solutions">Communications Solutions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=TE_Connectivity&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Communications Solutions" data-section="3" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>TE Connectivity's Communications Solutions segment supplies electronic components for home appliances, including products for washers, dryers, refrigerators, air conditioners, dishwashers, cooking appliances, water heaters, and microwaves.
</p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Transportation_Solutions">Transportation Solutions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=TE_Connectivity&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Transportation Solutions" data-section="4" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>The Transportation segment includes four business units: Automotive, Industrial &amp; Commercial Transportation, Application Tooling, and Sensor Solutions.
</p><p>TE's products are used by the automotive industry for body and chassis systems, convenience applications, driver information, infotainment, miniaturization, motor and powertrain applications, and safety and security systems. Hybrid and electronic mobility include in-vehicle technologies, battery technologies,<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup> and charging. In addition, TE's products are used for on- and off-highway vehicles and recreational transportation, including construction, agriculture, buses, and other vehicles.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup></p><p>TE offers sensors that are used by customers across multiple industries, including automotive, industrial equipment, commercial transportation, medical <a href="/wiki/Solution" title="Solution">solutions</a>, aerospace and defense, and consumer applications.
</p>
<h3 class="in-block"><span class="mw-headline" id="Industrial_Solutions">Industrial Solutions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=TE_Connectivity&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Industrial Solutions" data-section="5" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h3>
<p>The Industrial Solutions segment supplies products that connect and distribute power, data, and signals. Within the Industrial Solutions segment are the following business units: Industrial, Medical, Aerospace, Defense and Marine (AD&amp;M), and Energy.
</p><p>Products are used in factory automation and process control systems such as industrial controls, robotics, human machine interface, industrial communication, and power distribution. TE's intelligent building products are used to connect lighting, HVAC, elevators/escalators, and security. Its rail products are used in high-speed trains, metros, light rail vehicles, locomotives, and signaling switching equipment.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup> Also, its products are used by the solar and lighting industry.
</p><p>TE's products are used by the medical industry in imaging, diagnostic, therapeutic, surgical, tubing, and minimally invasive interventional applications.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup></p><p>Additionally, TE Connectivity provides components for the commercial aerospace industry<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup> from the initial stages of aircraft design to aftermarket support. TE's defense products include ruggedized electronic interconnects serving military aviation,<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup> marine, and ground vehicles including electronic warfare and space systems. Its oil and gas products include cables and electronics used for subsea environments in the offshore oil and gas and civil marine industries and in shipboard, subsea, and sonar applications. Also, TE's products are used by OEMs and utility companies in the electrical power industry and include solutions for the electrical power generation, transmission, distribution, and industrial markets.
</p>
