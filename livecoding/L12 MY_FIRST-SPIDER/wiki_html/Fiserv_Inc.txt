</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Fiserv&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1"><p>
Fiserv dates from July 31, 1984, from the merger of Sunshine State Systems, Inc. (<a href="/wiki/Tampa,_Florida" title="Tampa, Florida">Tampa, Florida</a>) and First Data Processing (<a href="/wiki/Milwaukee" title="Milwaukee">Milwaukee, Wisconsin</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> In 1986, Fiserv went public as a $70 million data processing company.<sup id="cite_ref-history_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-history-11">[11]</a></sup> Within years of becoming public, Fiserv was serving a number of <a href="/wiki/Financial_institution" title="Financial institution">financial institutions</a> in the United States.<sup id="cite_ref-history_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-history-11">[11]</a></sup> Fiserv employs a strategy of <a href="/wiki/Mergers_and_acquisitions" title="Mergers and acquisitions">corporate acquisition</a> and <a href="/wiki/Horizontal_integration" title="Horizontal integration">horizontal integration</a>. By 2009 it had become a <a href="/wiki/Fortune_500" title="Fortune 500">Fortune 500</a> company worth $4.7 billion with over 19,000 employees.<sup id="cite_ref-press_release_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-press_release-12">[12]</a></sup></p><div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Fiserv_offices_-_Hillsboro,_Oregon.JPG" class="image"><noscript><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/88/Fiserv_offices_-_Hillsboro%2C_Oregon.JPG/220px-Fiserv_offices_-_Hillsboro%2C_Oregon.JPG" decoding="async" width="220" height="159" class="thumbimage" data-file-width="2633" data-file-height="1897"></noscript><span class="lazy-image-placeholder" style="width: 220px;height: 159px;" data-src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/88/Fiserv_offices_-_Hillsboro%2C_Oregon.JPG/220px-Fiserv_offices_-_Hillsboro%2C_Oregon.JPG" data-alt="" data-width="220" data-height="159" data-class="thumbimage"> </span></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Fiserv_offices_-_Hillsboro,_Oregon.JPG" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Office in <a href="/wiki/Hillsboro,_Oregon" title="Hillsboro, Oregon">Hillsboro, Oregon</a>, formerly headquarters of Corillian</div></div></div>
