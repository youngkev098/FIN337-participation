<p><b>IDEX Corporation</b>, based in <a href="/wiki/Lake_Forest,_Illinois" title="Lake Forest, Illinois">Lake Forest, Illinois</a>, is a publicly traded company engaged in the development, design, and manufacture of <a href="/wiki/Fluidics" title="Fluidics">fluidics</a> systems and specialty engineered products. IDEX Corporation's products, which include pumps, clamping systems, flow meters, optical filters, powder processing equipment, hydraulic rescue tools, and fire suppression equipment, are used in a variety of industries ranging from <a href="/wiki/Agriculture" title="Agriculture">agriculture</a> to <a href="/wiki/Semiconductor_manufacturing" class="mw-redirect" title="Semiconductor manufacturing">semiconductor manufacturing</a>. It was formed when <a href="/wiki/Kohlberg_Kravis_Roberts" title="Kohlberg Kravis Roberts">Kohlberg Kravis Roberts</a> purchased several divisions of Houdaille Industries, which had recently been sold by KKR to <a href="/wiki/TI_Group" title="TI Group">Tube Instruments</a>.
