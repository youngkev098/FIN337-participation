</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Healthpeak_Properties&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p>The company was founded in 1985 with 2 acute care hospitals and 22 skilled nursing facilities.<sup id="cite_ref-change_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-change-2">[2]</a></sup></p><p>In 1985, the company became a <a href="/wiki/Public_company" title="Public company">public company</a> via an <a href="/wiki/Initial_public_offering" title="Initial public offering">initial public offering</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-quality_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quality-3">[3]</a></sup></p><p>Effective September 2007, the company changed its name from Health Care Property Investors, Inc. to HCP, Inc.<sup id="cite_ref-change_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-change-2">[2]</a></sup></p><p>In March 2008, the company was added to the <a href="/wiki/S%26P_500_Index" title="S&amp;P 500 Index">S&amp;P 500 Index</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p><p>In October 2016, the company completed the <a href="/wiki/Corporate_spin-off" title="Corporate spin-off">corporate spin-off</a> of Quality Care Properties.<sup id="cite_ref-quality_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quality-3">[3]</a></sup></p><p>In October 2019, the company restructured its <a href="/wiki/Joint_venture" title="Joint venture">joint venture</a> with <a href="/wiki/Brookdale_Senior_Living" title="Brookdale Senior Living">Brookdale Senior Living</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> The company also changed its name to Healthpeak Properties, Inc.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
