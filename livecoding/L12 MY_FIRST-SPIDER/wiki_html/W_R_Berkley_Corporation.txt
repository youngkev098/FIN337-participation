</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=W._R._Berkley_Corporation&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-wikimedia-edit-base20 edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p>W. R. Berkley Corporation was founded in 1967 by <a href="/wiki/William_R._Berkley" class="mw-redirect" title="William R. Berkley">William R. Berkley</a>, who currently serves as Executive Chairman.<sup id="cite_ref-14A_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14A-3">[3]</a></sup></p><p>In November 1973, the company became a <a href="/wiki/Public_company" title="Public company">public company</a> via an <a href="/wiki/Initial_public_offering" title="Initial public offering">initial public offering</a>. At that time, the founder owned 23.8% of the company.<sup id="cite_ref-14A_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14A-3">[3]</a></sup></p><p>In 1994, the company acquired Key Risk Management Services Inc.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p><p>In 1995, the company acquired the remaining 40% interest in Signet Star from <a href="/wiki/Gen_Re" title="Gen Re">Gen Re</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> The company also acquired MECC for $138 million.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p><p>In August 2006, the company acquired Garnet Captive Services LLC, a San Francisco insurance brokerage and alternative risk consulting firm.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p><p>On October 31, 2015, Mr. Berkley's son, W. Robert Berkley, Jr., became President and CEO.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
